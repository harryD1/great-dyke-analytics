About Us

Our mission at Great Dyke Analytics is to put our clients at the centre of geological data science innovation, allowing them to make significant mineral deposit discoveries faster and mine more efficiently.

We establish ourself as an innovator within the mineral exploration industry. Our first major product is a machine learning algorithm that is able to process lots of data points from a mining region to predict were commercially viable mineral deposits could be located (even in previously unexplored greenfield areas). For our second product, we mainly deal with the automation of mineral prospecting platforms. Our UAV Geophysics extension provides a complete workflow for planning and processing drone mineral exploration surveys for extracting information for accurate decision-making. For our final project, we specialize in Remote Sensing and Geophysics. Basically, we:

  • Capture geological data,
  • Catalog,
  • Connect and
  • Learn mineral trends.

Services

Services

AT Great Dyke Analytics, we offer the following services:

Research and Development

Our research has been focused on developing scalable solutions for mineral exploration, such as locating a prospective site with high confidence, thus lowering the cost & environmental impact of Drilling & Geological work

We find new mineral ore deposits

By creating intelligent models for extracting data and meaningful insights from geoscience imagery, we hope to make exploration activities far more successful than using traditional methods. In short, our algorithms consolidate images from any source, transforms them into understandable data and then connects them to the appropriate geological or mining activity.

We use drones for mineral exploration

Also known as UAVs, drones are changing the way we investigate the surface and subsurface of the Earth. At Great Dyke Analytics, our business is the efficient discovery of commercially viable mineral deposits, we design drone and multiple sensor systems for subsurface imaging. We develop solutions for gold, chromium and coal exploration.
