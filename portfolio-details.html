Project Details



Artificial Intelligence for Mineral Exploration



Overview

In Geology, there is a general reason which governs the formation of ore deposits. Machine Learning and Deep Learning algorithms link this reason to available mineral data-sets to determine the relationship. With that relationship, we can predict the location of economic mineral deposits in new exploration regions.

At Great Dyke Analytics, we use Artificial Intelligence algorithms to find new mineral ore deposits.

Benefits

  • AI can become smarter over time, thus increasing the efficiency.
  • Reduces exploration costs and enhances productivity
  • AI algorithms can potentially improve mineral discovery success rates in both brownfields and greenfields exploration.
  • Reduces the reliance on expert domain knowledge.
